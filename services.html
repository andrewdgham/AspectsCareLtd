Aspects Care

Aspects Care Ltd provides a range of high quality care services, including Domiciliary, Supported Living, Floating and Housing Related Support. These services can be provided to Adults and Older people with Autism, Challenging Behaviour, Dementia, Forensic Backgrounds, Learning Disabilities, Mental Health and Physical/Sensory Disabilities. We specialise in providing care that is highly focused on the needs of the indvidual and can tailor our services to provide the best and most appropriate care possible. We can provide anything between 1 and 24 hours of care and staffing to suit individual requirements. Our fully trained carers pay great attention to the individuals likes, dislikes, needs and ambitions in order to provide the best and most appropriate care possible. We are an established care provider in the UK, are contracted to Local Authority Social Care Departments and Clinical Commissioning Groups. We also undertake private contracts with individuals that require our services.
