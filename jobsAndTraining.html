Citizen involvment

rvice encourages Citizens to participate in monthly social activities and events in order to achieve positive outcomes such as overcoming isolation. The Citizen’s Involvement has also introduced a panel, in which citizens who access the service can air their views within the company in a more accessible way. This empowers the citizens to be more active in decisions which may affect them. There are several ways within the Citizens involvement department encourages citizens to be more involved in the company, such as citizen’s newsletters, citizens meetings, and citizen handouts.

Aspects Care was founded in 2004 with the long term intention of providing support and care services for clients who wish to live independently and in their own homes. Our philosophy is based upon "Care with a Difference".
